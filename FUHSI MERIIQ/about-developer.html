Meet the Developer

Built with Vision,
Intelligence & Precision.

My name is Moshood Moaz Akanji, and I designed, built, and wrote every part of FUHSI MeritIQ. I'm a frontend engineer, product designer, content writer, and agentic engineer — and at the time of building this, I'm also an active aspirant to FUHSI, the Federal University of Health Sciences, Ila-Orangun.

I didn't build this looking in from the outside. I went through the exact same frustration you might be feeling right now — a UTME score in hand, O'Level results ready, and no clean way to know whether I was genuinely competitive. The information was scattered, the calculations were confusing, and the answers were buried in WhatsApp groups. So I stopped waiting and built the tool I needed.

FUHSI MeritIQ does one thing well: it helps you calculate your aggregate accurately, see every course you qualify for, and face the admission process with real numbers — not guesswork.

Why only FUHSI? Admission rules aren't generic — every university has its own grading scale, aggregate formula, and cut-off marks. I know FUHSI's process because I'm inside it. Trying to cover every Nigerian university would mean covering none of them properly. I chose depth over breadth, and I'd rather give one school something accurate than give every school something vague.
