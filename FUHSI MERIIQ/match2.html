How FUHSI Calculates Your Aggregate
The 3 documented formulas behind this report
Method 1 · Legacy 80:20
80% UTME + 20% O'Level
O'Level scale: A1=4 · B2=3.5 · B3=3 · C4=2 · C5=2.5 · C6=1, out of 20 points across your 5 core subjects. Observed roughly three admission cycles ago.
Method 2 · Current 80:20 Most Recent
80% UTME + 20% O'Level
Updated O'Level scale: A1=6 · B2=5 · B3=4 · C4=3 · C5=2 · C6=1, out of 30 points. This is the formula FUHSI used in the last admission cycle.
Method 3 · 75:25 Variant Unconfirmed
75% UTME + 25% O'Level
Same O'Level scale as Method 1, but shifts more weight onto O'Level performance. Observed about two admission cycles ago, never officially confirmed.

FUHSI has not published one permanent aggregate formula — the weighting and O'Level scale have changed across recent admission cycles. That's why this report calculates your aggregate all three documented ways, instead of assuming a single method, and shows you the full realistic range.
