❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using FUHSI MeritIQ

How is the FUHSI aggregate score calculated?
+

The Federal University of Health Sciences, Ila-Orangun (FUHSI) calculates its admission aggregate using an 80:20 or 75:25 ratio system, combining your UTME (JAMB) score and your O'Level (WAEC/NECO) grades.

Does MeritIQ guarantee my admission?
+

No. MeritIQ is an intelligence and prediction tool — it does not guarantee admission. Our predictions are based on historical data and current trends, but FUHSI's final admission decisions are made independently by the university.

How accurate are the admission predictions?
+

Our model has historically been accurate within a small margin for most courses. Accuracy depends on data availability, course demand shifts, and special admissions categories. Medicine and Nursing predictions tend to be most accurate due to larger data sets.

What are alternative course recommendations?
+

If your scores don't meet the threshold for your preferred course, MeritIQ suggests related FUHSI courses where your aggregate score has a higher likelihood of success, based on cutoff trends and subject compatibility.

How often is FUHSI admission data updated?
+

We update our data as soon as FUHSI releases new admission information, including Post-UTME dates, cutoff scores, and policy changes. Users can also subscribe to real-time notifications for instant updates.
